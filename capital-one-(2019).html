Dwell Time Analysis

Dwell time was approximately 117 days (March 22 to July 17, 2019), from initial access to detection, due to misconfigured WAF and lack of cloud monitoring.

Dwell Time Insight: The dwell time (117 days vs. 2019 median ~56 days) was prolonged due to misconfigured cloud infrastructure and no monitoring. Detection relied on an external tip, exposing cloud security gaps. Swift containment prevented further exfiltration.
