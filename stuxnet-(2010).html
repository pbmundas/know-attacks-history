Dwell Time Analysis

Dwell time was approximately 12-18 months (early 2009 to June 2010), the period Stuxnet operated undetected in Natanz’s air-gapped systems. This long duration enabled reconnaissance, PLC reprogramming, and physical sabotage.

Dwell Time Insight: Stuxnet’s long dwell time was like a silent saboteur in a factory, unnoticed until damage was done. Experts: Median dwell time in 2010 was ~180 days (per Mandiant); Stuxnet’s 12-18 months was exceptional due to its stealth and air-gap targeting.
