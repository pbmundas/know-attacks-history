Dwell Time Analysis

Dwell time was approximately 1 week (April 24 to May 1, 2024), from detection to public disclosure, due to swift containment but delayed scope confirmation.

Dwell Time Insight: The dwell time (~1 week vs. 2024 median ~21 days) was short due to internal detection, but lack of MFA allowed initial access. Swift response prevented further exfiltration, but delayed disclosure amplified reputational damage.
