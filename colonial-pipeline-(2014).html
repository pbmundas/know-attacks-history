Dwell Time Analysis

Dwell time was approximately 8 days (April 29 to May 7, 2021), the period attackers operated undetected in Colonial’s network, allowing data theft and ransomware deployment.

Step 1: Initial Access (April 29) Step 2: Lateral Movement (April 29-May 6) Step 3: Collection/Exfiltration (April 29-May 6) Step 4: Ransomware Deployment (May 6) Step 5: Detection (May 7)
Compromised VPN password (no MFA).
What went wrong: Password reuse, no 2FA.		 Moved laterally using stolen credentials.
Factor: No network segmentation.		 Stole 100GB of data; prepared ransomware.
Factor: No DLP or anomaly detection.		 Deployed ransomware, locking IT systems.
Factor: No real-time malware detection.		 Detected after systems locked; pipeline shut down.
Total time: Disrupted critical infrastructure.

Dwell Time Insight: The dwell time was like a thief slipping through an unlocked gate, unnoticed due to weak authentication. Experts: Median dwell time in 2021 was ~21 days (Mandiant); Colonial’s 8 days was short but impactful due to critical infrastructure targeting.
