Dwell Time Analysis

Dwell time was approximately 6–9 months (from spring 2015 to detection on December 23, 2015), allowing attackers to infiltrate, map networks, and stage malware undetected.

Dwell Time Insight: The dwell time was like a saboteur covertly wiring explosives in a factory for months, unnoticed due to poor security. Industry reports suggest median dwell times in 2015 were ~150–200 days; the Ukrainian grid’s 6–9 months was prolonged, enabling a highly coordinated attack on critical infrastructure.
