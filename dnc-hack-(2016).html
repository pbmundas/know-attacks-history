Dwell Time Analysis

Dwell time was approximately 10 months for Cozy Bear (June 2015–April 2016) and 7 months for Fancy Bear (September 2015–April 2016), allowing extensive data theft and preparation for leaks.

Step 1: Initial Access (Jun/Sep 2015) Step 2: Lateral Movement (Sep 2015–Mar 2016) Step 3: Collection/Exfiltration (Sep 2015–Mar 2016) Step 4: Data Leakage (Jun–Jul 2016) Step 5: Detection (Apr 2016)
Spear-phishing compromised credentials.

Factor: No DLP or anomaly detection.		 Leaked data via Guccifer 2.0, WikiLeaks.
Factor: No exfiltration monitoring.		 Detected via unusual activity; CrowdStrike engaged.
Total time: Enabled election interference.

Dwell Time Insight: The dwell time was like spies infiltrating a campaign headquarters for months, unnoticed due to poor defenses. Industry reports suggest median dwell times in 2016 were ~150 days; the DNC’s 7–10 months was prolonged, enabling significant data theft and strategic leaks.
